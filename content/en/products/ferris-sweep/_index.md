## Introduction

The Ferris Sweep is the class ergonomic keyboard design.

- **Layout**: 34-key ortholinear split keyboard with customizable layouts
- **Connectivity**: Two-mode connectivity (Bluetooth, and USB-C) and easy to switch
- **Case Material**: 3D printed cases with user custom graphics
