# Support Resources

Need help with your NXTKB keyboard? We're here to assist you.

## Contact Options

### Online Support
- **Documentation**: Comprehensive help articles and guides
- **Troubleshooting**: Common issues and solutions
- **Community Forum**: Connect with other users and enthusiasts

### Direct Support
- **Email Support**: Send your questions to [support@nxtkb.com](mailto:support@nxtkb.com)
- **Live Chat**: Available during business hours (PST) on our online store
- **Remote Troubleshooting**: We can help configure your keyboard settings

## Warranty Information

### Standard Warranty Coverage
- **Ferris Sweep**: 2-year limited warranty
- **Ferris Sweep Pro**: 3-year limited warranty
- Covers manufacturing defects and build quality issues

### What's Covered
- Keyboard halves (keys, controllers, battery)
- Wireless functionality
- Manufacturing defects
- Issues during normal usage

### What's Not Covered
- Physical damage from misuse or accidents
- Normal wear and tear
- Damage during shipping (file claim with carrier)
- Modifications by the user
