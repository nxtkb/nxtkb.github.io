## Introduction

The Ferris Sweep Pro is the ultimate expression of ergonomic keyboard design. Combining cutting-edge technology with premium materials, this professional-grade keyboard offers the highest level of customization and comfort for users who demand the absolute best.

- **Layout**: 34-key ortholinear split keyboard with customizable layouts
- **Connectivity**: Two-mode connectivity (Bluetooth, and USB-C) and easy to switch
- **Case Material**: 3D printed cases with user custom graphics
